DESCRIPTION

Native to Asia, this yellow Indian spice is a member of the ginger family that possesses a gingery-ness that is often described as woody, musky, and floral & one of the highly prized spices in the world for its flavor and color. Turmeric often used as a dye for cloth, has become a staple for curries, soups, cakes and numerous other foods throughout Africa, Asia, and Europe. Ground turmeric powder imbues food with a peculiar flavor unique to turmeric.
